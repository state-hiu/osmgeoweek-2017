OSM Geography Awareness Week

Nov 13-19, 2016

Teachers, students, community groups, map lovers, in the US and around the world, join together to celebrate geography and make maps with OpenStreetMap, the free and openly editable map of the world.

Find an event Submit an event

A global coalition of partners are hosting mapping events at colleges, community centers and other institutions to map places around the globe.

On the local level, grassroots organizers around the world are holding over {{event_counter}} events at local schools and community centers during #osmgeoweek. If you are organizing an event, or even just thinking about it, get in touch at mapgive@state.gov.

For more details about Geography Awareness Week, its history, and more information about this year’s theme, “Explore! The Power of Parks” take a look at National Geographic’s Geography Awareness Week homepage. Follow #osmgeoweek for updates during the week, and share your experiences using the #osmgeoweek hashtag.
